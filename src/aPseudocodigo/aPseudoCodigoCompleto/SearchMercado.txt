*Search on mercadolibre.com.mx*

Pre-conditions
	Electronic device with internet connection (pc, mobile, tablet)
	Web browser installed on the electronic device (Chrome, Firefox, IE, Edge)


Steps:
	1. Open the web browser
	2. Type in or copy the url https://www.mercadolibre.com.mx/ on the url navigation bar
	3. Wait for the page to load
	4. Click on the Search field
	5. Enter the search criteria
	6. Click enter
	7. Wait for the results page to load
	8. Verify the list displayed contains the product you searched for on step 5
